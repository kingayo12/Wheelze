Investments

We will make your Money work for you!

Whatever your financial goals might be as an individual or corporate investor, our tailored low-risk investment products have been designed to help you achieve those goals. Get started by selecting your desired investment product and earn guaranteed returns far superior to the market average.

  • Target Investment Plan
  • Fixed Investment Note
  • Secured Income Plan
  • Instant Value Note
  • Quest Vault
  • Platinum Note

Target Investment Plan

This is a goal-oriented investment product that helps investors build savings culture through making periodic contributions towards set goal(s). With this product, investors are able to save either daily, weekly, fortnightly, monthly, or at such other intervals towards desired targets. The investment goal or target might be a car, house, wedding, rent, business, education, vacation, retirement, and such other life’s ambitions.

  • Suitable for investors planning towards defined future goals, which will be stated from inception;
  • Minimum starting amount of N50,000 with subsequent contributions of a minimum of N10,000;
  • Minimum tenor of 3 months with the flexibility to determine how long and how much to save;
  • Both the principal and accrued interest are available for withdrawal at the set target end date;
  • Liquidation before the stated target end date is possible, but subject to pre-liquidation fee charged on interest only.
Start Investing

Fixed Investment Note

The most notable features of this product are its simplicity and flexibility. Investment in the product requires the commitment of an amount for a specified period after which the committed amount (principal) and accrued interest are paid in lump sum to the investor. The investor only needs to invest and watch on as the money grows consistently over the chosen period.

  • Suitable for passive investors seeking to invest one-off in return for a bulk payment at maturity;
  • Minimum investment amount of N1,000,000 which may be toped-up at any time without restriction;
  • Minimum tenor of 1 month and a maximum of 1-year renewable;
  • Interest rate is fixed over the period and the accumulated amount can be rolled over at maturity;
  • Investment could be liquidated before maturity, subject to pre-liquidation fee charged on interest only.
Start Investing

Secured Income Plan

This product pays guaranteed or steady income to investors throughout the period of the investment. The income is paid at regular intervals, which could be weekly, monthly, quarterly, annually or as may otherwise be desired by the investor. There is an option of reinvesting the periodic income in qualified products, thereby further enhancing the investor’s earnings through interest capitalization.

  • Suitable for investors who desire regular or periodic income on their investments;
  • Minimum investment amount of N100,000 with no maximum limit;
  • Minimum tenor of 3 months and a maximum of 1-year renewable;
  • Interest rate is fixed and the investment amount can be rolled over upon maturity;
  • Investment could be liquidated before maturity subject to pre-liquidation fee charged on interest only.
Start Investing

Instant Value Note

This product delivers immediate value to investors by paying upfront interest on their investment while the principal amount gets paid at maturity. With this product, there is no point having to wait till the pay day as investors are able to withdraw the upfront interest immediately, reinvest it instantly in another product, or hold it in their (interest-earning) account for other transactional purposes.

  • Suitable for investors seeking instant (one-off) returns on their investments to meet immediate needs;
  • Minimum investment amount of N500,000 with no maximum limit;
  • Minimum tenor of 3 months and a maximum of 1-year renewable;
  • Principal amount could be paid at maturity or rolled over for another term;
  • Investment could be liquidated before maturity subject to pre-liquidation fee charged on principal.
Start Investing

Quest Vault

Investing towards future goals requires a lot of discipline and oftentimes, we react emotionally to our investment plans and take decisions based on our current mood. The QuestVault product is designed to enforce financial discipline in investors, wherein the investment amount is locked up and only accessible for withdrawal after the date specified by the investor from the outset.

  • Suitable for investors with mid-term goals and who desire to avoid the temptation of early withdrawal;
  • Both principal and accrued interest are only available for withdrawal at the specified maturity date;
  • Minimum Investment amount of N50,000 with no maximum limit;
  • Minimum tenor of 1 month with rollover option at maturity;
  • Liquidation before maturity is discouraged and in exceptional cases where this happens, the investor will forfeit the full accrued interest.
Start Investing

Platinum Note

The Platinum Note is a specialised investment product designed to meet the capital appreciation and income targets of mid to long-term investors. Typically, this product offers a much superior rate of return compared to the average return on similar low-risk instruments in the fixed income market.

  • Suitable for High Net-worth Individuals (HNIs) and institutional investors;
  • Minimum investment amount of N50.0million with option to create multiple tranches;
  • Minimum tenor of 1 year and a maximum of 5 years renewable;
  • Interest income is payable either monthly, quarterly or annually but with both capitalization and withdrawal options;
  • Investment can only be liquidated after 6 months for a tenor of 1 year, and after 1 year for a tenor of 2 years or more;
  • Such liquidation before maturity date is subject to pre-liquidation fee charged on interest only.
Start Investing

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I Invest?

Please follow this process:

  1. Download our Mobile App on Google Play or the App Store and set up your account in minutes. Alternatively, you can set up your account on FundQuest Online or by dialing *5078# on your mobile phone. Your Unique Account Number will be generated immediately
  2. Transfer funds to your Unique Account Number from any of your bank’s Mobile, Web, USSD Code, ATM or by adding your debit card to your account. Your Account with FundQuest will be credited immediately
  3. Choose the investment product you are interest in and how long you want to invest for. You may also send an email to contact@fundquestnigeria.com instructing us to debit your account and set up your investment
  4. We will complete the setup of your investment immediately and automatically send you an Investment Certificate
  5. You will subsequently be able to track all your investments and returns. You will also be able to withdraw your returns and principal or reinvest them to keep growing your money
What do I need to open an Account?

Just your BVN, Selfie/Picture, Government ID, and Utility Bill. You can scan the documents with your phone and upload to our Mobile App (both Android and iOS), Internet Banking, or send to us via email at contact@fundquestnigeria.com

How do I select which product is right for me?

All our investment products are tailored to meet a specific goal:

If you’d like to regularly set aside money for the sole aim of growing your wealth or investment portfolio, the Fixed Investment Note is right for you. You can make additions to your investment at your own convenience while maintaining existing terms on your investment.

If you’re planning towards a project such as a car, house, education, rent, wedding, vacation or similar goals, the Target Investment Plan is a great option. You are allowed to set up a plan, which could be daily, weekly, monthly, etc., and make periodic contributions to your investment account in order to fulfil your pre-determined goal.

If you’re searching for a mid to long-term investment plan that pays high returns periodically and preserve your capital, the Platinum Note is perfect for you. You are certain of earning very strong returns consistently while enjoying the pleasure of investing.

If you’re searching for an investment product that can enforce savings discipline and guard against the temptation of depleting your investment, the Quest Vault is perfect in assisting you meet this goal. We will help you lock up the fund till its maturity, when you harvest both your investment and a good return.

If you’re searching for an investment product that can pay your interest upfront or immediately you invest, the Instant Value Note is a wise choice. We will credit your account immediately while you either withdraw or reinvest your return.

If you’re searching for a short-term investment product that pays you guaranteed returns periodically (monthly, quarterly, or such other frequencies), the Secured Income Plan is designed just for you.

What is the minimum amount I can invest?

You can start investing with a minimum of N50,000. You can then contribute any amount to your investment thereafter.

How is my interest paid?

It depends on your choice of investment products. We calculate and accrue interest on your investment daily and will credit the interest to your account at the end of every month. Depending on choice of product and frequency of interest payment, we can pay your interest upfront, monthly, quarterly, semi-annually, annually or at maturity of the investment.

Are my investment returns guaranteed?

Absolutely yes; whatever returns stated on your Investment Certificate or Advice are fixed and guaranteed.

How safe is my money?

FundQuest is licensed and regulated by the Central Bank of Nigeria (CBN). Funds saved with us are invested in credit creation and low-risk money market instruments. We also retain part of the funds in cash to enable us meet up with your withdrawal requests promptly and on demand. We carefully carry out due diligence and credibility profiling on all our investment partners, while we also maintain strong internal governance on the management of our investments.

How secure are your online channels?

All our digital channels (Mobile, Web, USSD) are very secure for you to transact. We deploy bank-grade encryption technology across all our online platforms, with your personal information encrypted and stored securely. We also do not hold card details; they are stored with our card payment partner, a PCI-DSS compliant payment processor. 

How easily can I withdraw or liquidate my investment?

You can withdraw part of your investment or fully liquidate the investment anytime and without notice. Just send an email to contact@fundquestnigeria.com or activate the withdrawal/liquidation form on your FundQuest Mobile, FundQuest Online or *5078#. We will process your withdrawal/liquidation instruction promptly and credit your account same day.

What are your fees and charges?

We do not charge any fee or penalty on your investment proceeds at maturity. But if you withdraw your investment before the stated maturity date, a certain percentage of your interest (which will be indicated on your Investment Certificate) will be withheld as penalty for pre-liquidating your investment. But note that under no circumstance will your principal investment be penalized.
